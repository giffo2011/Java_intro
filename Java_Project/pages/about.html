It all started with a golfball...

Back in 1983 Khaleed Mukmar came to America with nothing but the clothes on his back, a golfball, and a dream. A dream to create the most engaging miniature golf course in the world. He succeeded. Today His dream has been fully realized with the sprawling 4 acre course that is Golf Adventure. With 18 holes, a minature castle, and a gift shop that doubles as hole 14, this course has something for everyone. Whether you are a child or a child-at-heart.
